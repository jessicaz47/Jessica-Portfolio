About Me

Jessica Zonta



Born in Stoney Creek, Ontario, I moved to Toronto when I was 18 for university. Upon graduating, I moved out to Alberta as one of the founders of a vodka cooler company. Since then I have developed an interest in business and startups.

Last summer I started working on travel app idea of my own. With the project currently on pause with the current state of the trtavel industry, I started doing UX/UI design for a friend's e-commerce startup. Thoroughly enjoying this new skill, I decided to further my knowledge in the field by enrolling in a coding bootcamp. In the short term, I plan to use this knowledge to take on a Product Manager role within the growing company I am working for. Once the travel industry returns to normal, I plan on using my newfound skills to make an MVP for my app.
